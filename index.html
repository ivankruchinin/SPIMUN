About Us

Who We Are

SPIMUN is a two-day simulation of the United Nations that will take place on the 20th and 21st of March, 2026 at St. Peter's International School, in Palmela, Portugal. At SPIMUN, delegates will debate the most pressing issues in The Human Rights Council and The Economic and Social Council.

Striving for innovation and empowerment of young delegates and their knowledge of international relations and diplomacy, we are proud to announce the fourth annual edition of SPIMUN!
